Even if this project addresses mental health during COVID-19, these are some of the factors that must be considered. Take care of yourself and the loved ones following some simple steps as mentioned below.

Wash Your Hands

Washing hands regularly with medical handwashes whenever you go out or have a physical contact with anything reduces chances of getting Coronavirus by 80%

Wear Face Mask

Wear your mask regularly whenever you go out. This reduces chances being caught by Coronavirus. People who wear masks are less likely to suffer from COVID-19.

Use Sanitizer

Using medically prescribed sanitizers will kill all the particles of Coronavirus, if present on hands and prevent Coronavirus from entering to the lungs

Practice Social Distancing

Maintaining 6-feet distance from everyone is scientifically proven to be effective to prevent the spread of COVID-19
